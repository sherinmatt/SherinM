Roswell

Collaborators: Mariam Khan, Chandni Patel, Abdullah Sakar, Manan Shah, Sandra George, Sheroz Khan, Jonathan Carangui

Background Information: For this Rutgers Capstone work, the main goal was to employ agile methodologies and design thinking to research and design a healthcare technology tool with Capstone team members- under Johnson & Johnson staff's supervision. Conducted market research analysis, experimentation and created a Business Model Canvas for a healthcare solution that would allow psychiatrists to make faster and accurate diagnoses by cross-referencing a patient's acute symptoms with EMR (Electronic Medical Records). Utilized Trello Board, Kanban methodology.

Capstone Objective: Creating a healthcare solution,“Rosewell”, that will aid psychiatrist in making efficient and accurate diagnosis so as to reduce healthcare costs and time

Tasks Undertaken - Market Research -> Our Solution (RoseWell Prototype) -> Data Strategy -> Financial Plan

For the Capstone, my focus was on the Market Research section of the Capstone work. The images below show detailed Market Research steps undertaken for the healthcare solution, Roswell

Market Research: Stakeholders include Android and Apple app store, Electronic Medical Record System, Psychiatrists, Healthcare companies, Hospitals, Patients

Market Research: Potential Partners include Epic, GE Healthcare, Cerner, CareCloud, AthenaHealth
